Built for
the Thrill

Unleash the true potential of the new Vios through a host of features that deliver exhilaration on a whole new level.

  • New dual vvt-i engine
  • New CVT with 7-Speed Sport Sequential Shiftmatic Mode
  • Vehicle Stability Control (VSC) and Traction Control (TRC)
  • New 1.5GX
  • Refreshed 1.5TRD
  • New Exterior Colours

Available in all variants, this innovative engine ensures that you will experience a fantastic drive, every day. Overall fuel efficiency and performance have been improved while CO₂ emissions is reduced. Your drive ahead will definitely be a smooth and quiet one.

7-speed manual shifts is now possible with the availability of an M mode +/- in the new Vios. You simply won’t get enough of its smooth driving performance and excellent fuel efficiency during normal driving conditions. Whenever you need to turn up the drive, quick and short shifts will let you experience a powerful, linear acceleration.

*Available for all variants except for 1.5J (MT).

VSC effectively controls and stabilizes vehicle to prevent oversteer or understeer when cornering while TRC adjusts and distributes power to individual wheels for superior grip. VSC now available in all variants.
